Shiyam Galyon is a Syrian American writer from Houston, Texas, and the incoming communications coordinator for the War Resisters League in New York City. She is an Arabic heritage speaker studying modern standard Arabic in an effort to make antiwar communication campaigns accessible to a wider audience.
