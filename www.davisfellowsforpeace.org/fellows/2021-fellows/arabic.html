Neggeen Sadid

Year of Fellowship: 
2021
School: 
Arabic
Area of Interest: 
—

Neggeen Sadid is a cartographer and writer. Interested in Arabic poetry, as well as the culture, history political traditions of the Middle East, she hopes to use knowledge of Arabic to read poems by Mahmoud Darwish in the original. She is also interested in the affinities between Arabic and Farsi. Previously, she worked on researching the spread of disinformation targetting humanitarians, on online harms, and on victims rights issues.

Salvatore Vicari is Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors Without Borders) humanitarian affairs advisor in the Middle East. Zane Wolfang

Year of Fellowship: 
2021
School: 
Arabic
Area of Interest: 
—

Zane Wolfang has worked extensively in multiple countries including Palestine and Iraq as an ultimate frisbee coach, consulting on development projects and running ultimate frisbee clinics for Arabic-speaking players, coaches, and teachers. He also works as a journalist, focusing not only on social and political issues but also on cultural and human-interest stories in the Middle East and North Africa. He intends to return to the Middle East in 2022 to continue building and supporting new ultimate frisbee programs in Palestine and across the region.

Lucy Towers

2021

Arabic

Lucy Towers is a broadcast journalist and producer who has worked across the Middle East for the last decade. She works for Channel 4 News in London, producing short documentaries on unreported stories from around the world. As well as continuing to report from the region, she hopes to pursue a master’s in war and psychiatry with an emphasis on childhood trauma and memory.

Sophia Schroeder

Year of Fellowship: 
2021
School: 
Arabic
Area of Interest: 
—

Sophia Schroeder is a PhD student at UCL in London, focusing on the role of domestic courts in consociational systems of power-sharing, such as in Lebanon and Northern Ireland. She seeks to understand how courts contribute to conflict management and resolution in deeply divided societies, in particular in post-conflict societies.
